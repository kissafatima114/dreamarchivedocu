About the Artist

Kissa Fatima is a Digital Futures student at OCAD University working across interactive media, web-based environments, and projection. Her work explores memory, atmosphere, and familiar spaces that feel slightly unstable or difficult to locate. She is interested in how digital interfaces can function as emotional environments rather than purely informational systems.

Dream Archive is part of her ongoing exploration of how memory can be experienced visually through interaction and spatial navigation within browser-based environments.
