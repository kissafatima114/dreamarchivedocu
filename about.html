About the Artist

Kissa Fatima is a Digital Futures student at OCAD University working across interactive media and web-based environments. Her work focuses on creating interactive digital experiences that are visually engaging and invite users to explore, respond, and connect with the content in different ways. She enjoys making user-interactive work that combines creativity, atmosphere, and experimentation across digital media.

Dream Archive is part of her ongoing exploration of how memory can be experienced visually through interaction and spatial navigation within browser-based environments.
